Nutshell

Nutshell lets you put "expandable explanations" in your writing, like this! You can even make these explanations :recursive.

Why do this? Well, if you were to explain something to me in-person, I can ask "wait, what's that word mean", "could you break down that idea", "tell me more!", etc. But by default, writing is one-size-fits-all. Nutshell doesn't solve that completely, but it lets your reader tailor your writing — a lil' bit — to their prior knowledge, interests, and curiosity.

(Why not just use links? Because those break the flow of reading, and your reader usually doesn't need everything in that link, just a section. Nutshell lets you embed "just a section", and if you :click this Nutshell, you can see how it also preserves flow of reading!)
